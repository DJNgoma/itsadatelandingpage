FAQ

Is the app available yet?

Yes — It's a Date is live on the App Store for iPhone. Download it here.

Will Android be supported?

Eventually. We're focused on shipping iOS first. Leave your email above and we'll let you know when Android lands.

Can I just say the date out loud?

Yes. The app supports voice input, so you can say something like “Next Friday at 7pm” and let it fill in the plan.

Do I need to connect my calendar?

Yes — checking availability is the whole point. The app uses Apple's EventKit and reads only locally.

Will you spam me?

No. You'll get one email when there's something to tell you, and that's it.

Why keep an email form if the app is already live?

It's there for Android availability and occasional product updates. iPhone downloads happen directly through the App Store.
